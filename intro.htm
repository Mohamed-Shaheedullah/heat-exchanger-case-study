Introduction to task

An economizer is a vessel which uses 'waste' heat to pre-heat boiler feedwater, thereby reducing the heat input required in the boiler itself.

On nitric acid plants, the waste heat is provided by hot, process gases, resulting from the oxidation of ammonia in air, prior to their absorption in water to form nitric acid.

The economizers in question are vertical, shell-and-tube heat exchangers, with the boiler feedwater on the 'shell' side, and the hot gases flowing through the tubes.

Your task will be to select materials for parts of the heat exchanger

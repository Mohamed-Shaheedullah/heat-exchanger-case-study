Case Study

During this exercise you will:

  • gain an understanding of materials selection methodology
  • answer a series of questions on materials and properties
  • predict failure mechanisms
  • select materials for a specified environment
  • see the effect of cost

Answering Questions

The questions are of the following types:

  • single selection from a list
  • multiple selections from a list
  • promote and demote points to an ordered list

The method of reaching the correct answer is the same for all:

  1. Make your initial selections then click the 'submit' button
  2. The response will be for the first incorrect answer only
  3. Change this answer then click 'submit' again
  4. Repeat until all correct
